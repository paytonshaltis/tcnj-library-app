TCNJ Library App Summary

This application was developed during a capstone experience in Computer Science at The College of New Jersey by Art Malarcyzk and Jon Stoeber. Jack Jamieson assisted with artwork. The project continues to evolve and grow with other Computer Science research students.

Contributions

Updates to this application were implemented during a capstone experience in Computer Science at The College of New Jersey by Frank Corradi, assisted by Brandon Gottlob, Ken Whittaker (Spring 2013), Warren Seto (Fall 2014), and Madison Martin (Fall 2016).

Dual-platform implementation of this application was performed in a capstone experience at The College of New Jersey by David Quadragesimus Shull, assisted by Sophia Goldberg (Spring 2017).

Study room occupancy detection was added at The College of New Jersey by Sophia Goldberg, assisted by Randell Corrido and Victoria Swartz (Fall 2017 - Spring 2018).

Backend development and data filtering done by Ryan Bogutz (2018-2019).

Accessibility, room scheduling algorithms and module design, and database enhancement added by Jenna Oak (2018-2019).

Room scheduling integration implemented by Gabrielle Curcio (2018-2019).

Production rollout, server support, accuracy of occupancy detection, computer availability upgrade completed by Mark Meddleton (2019-2020), assisted by Allison Russell (2019), Lisa Walker (2019-2020), and Jenna Stiesi (2020).

Application WiFi stabilization, updated computer mappings, and additional backend development including serviced computers and error reporting implemented by Payton Shaltis (Fall 2021).
