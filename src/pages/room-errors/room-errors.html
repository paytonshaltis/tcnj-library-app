Incorrect Study Room Data?

How We Collect Data

The TCNJ Library App reports study room occupancy data based on the readings of motion sensors in each room. Inconsistencies between the app and the TCNJ Library are likely due to the sensitivity of these sensors.

False Positives

If students remain very still in a study room, it is possible that the motion sensors will indicate that a room is unoccupied, even if there truly are students in the room. Once motion is detected again, the app will begin displaying accurate data.

False Negatives

If students pass by a sensitive motion sensor, the app may indicate that the room is occupied, even if there are no students in the room. After some time, the motion sensor will fail to detect motion and the app will begin displaying accurate data.
