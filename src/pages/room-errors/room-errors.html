Incorrect Study Room Data?

How We Collect Data

The TCNJ Library App reports study room occupancy data based on the readings of motion sensors in each room.

False Positives

If students remain very still in a study room, it is possible that the motion sensors will indicate that a room is unoccupied, even if there truly are students in the room.

False Negatives

If students pass by a sensitive motion sensor, the app may indicate that the room is occupied, even if there are no students in the room.

Other Errors?

If you do happen to notice something else that you believe to be an error with the TCNJ Library App, please feel free to let us know!
