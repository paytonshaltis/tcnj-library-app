What is the TCNJ VPN?

The TCNJ VPN is a way to access the TCNJ network without needing to be on campus. While connected to the VPN, you can access the online features of the TCNJ Library App wherever you go! More information about the VPN can be found in the IT Knowledge Base.

How can I connect to the TCNJ VPN?
