Incorrect Computer Data?

How We Collect Data

The TCNJ Library App reports computer occupancy data based on which computers are currently logged in at the time. Inconsistencies between the app and the TCNJ Library are likely due to students failing to sign into or out of computers.

False Positives

If students are sitting at a computer but have not logged in, the app will indicate that this computer is unoccupied, even if there is a student at the computer. We encourage students to sit elsewhere if they are not using the computers so that other students may use them.

False Negatives

If students fail to sign out of a computer, the app will indicate that this computer is occupied, even though there is no student actively using the machine. We encourage students to sign out of computers when they are done using them in order to keep occupancy data as accurate as possible.

Other Errors?
