Incorrect Computer Data?

How We Collect Data

The TCNJ Library App reports computer occupancy data based on which computers are currently logged in at the time.

False Positives

If students are sitting at a computer but have not logged in, the app will indicate that this computer is unoccupied, even if there is a student at the computer.

False Negatives

If students fail to sign out of a computer, the app will indicate that this computer is occupied, even though there is no student actively using the machine.

Other Errors?

If you do happen to notice something else that you believe to be an error with the TCNJ Library App, please feel free to let us know!
